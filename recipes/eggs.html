Scrambled Eggs

Learn how to make delicious scrambled eggs with just the right texture!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Chili Powder
  • (Optional) Butter

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a non-stick pan or place butter into a pan
  2. Cook on stovetop on low heat
  3. Add salt, garlic, and chili powder
  4. Mix and break apart eggs, make sure nothing is sticking to pan
  5. Cook until they are somewhat hardened, make sure they are somewhat gooey
  6. Turn off head
  7. Let them sit in hot pan until they are the texture you want
  8. Enjoy!
