Patty Melt

Egg and Beef with Cheese

This is a delicious patty melt, made with exquisite cheese and cooked just right with juicy beef. Yum!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Egg
  • Beef
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Butter two sides of any bread
  2. Place beef on bread
  3. Place cheese on beef
  4. Place any other ingredients on cheese
  5. Place second slice of buttered bread on top
  6. Place in pan and cook on stovetop until both sides of bread are cooked and cheese is melted
  7. Simultaneously, cook egg in separate pan
  8. Open cooked patty melt and place cooked egg on top
  9. Enjoy!
